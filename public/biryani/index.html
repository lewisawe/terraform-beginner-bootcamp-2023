Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Meat (chicken, mutton, or vegetables)
  • Basmati Rice
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves)
  • Yogurt
  • Herbs (cilantro, mint)
  • Onions
  • Ghee or Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Saffron or Food Color

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, spices, and herbs. Allow it to marinate for a few hours or overnight.
  2. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices until partially cooked.
  3. Layer marinated meat and partially cooked rice in a pot.
  4. Cook the biryani using the dum method to meld the flavors together.
  5. Garnish with fried onions, fresh herbs, and saffron soaked in milk or food color.
  6. Serve hot with raita and salad.
